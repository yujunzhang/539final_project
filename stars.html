He Hua

In 2023, panda He Hua (also known as Hua Hua) in the major social media platforms burst into flames, "Panda He Hua" has been on a number of platforms hot search first, and a long time to dominate the list, known as "panda world of the top flow of female stars". At the end of the queue, there is a sign that reads: "It takes more than 2 hours to queue up here to see Hua Hua".
Hua Hua's appearance is very recognizable, and fans can always easily find her among the many pandas. She is smaller than pandas of her age, chubby and round; her head is so integrated with her body that her neck is almost invisible; and she sits down like a triangle, which makes her a true round "roller".

Meng Lan

December 15, 2021 at 11:30, the Beijing Zoo giant panda Meng Lan with their own strength successfully attracted attention. He climbed to the buffer zone outside the stadium on their own, causing tourists to crowd around.
Some tourists witnessed the whole process of Meng Lan turning out of the fence. It was reported that Meng Lan stepped on the red ball inside the stadium, grabbed the spray line, and then climbed over the fence.
Menglan turned out of the fence, causing tourists to gather around and take pictures. Good thing it just lying on the wall, visiting tourists, and no further other action. Park staff quickly rushed to the scene to evacuate the tourists, keepers with food to lure the panda turned out of the fence to lead back.

Fu Bao

As the first giant panda born in Korea, "Fu Bao" is loved by the local people and has many lovely nicknames such as "Longren Fushi", "Princess Fu" and "Fudun Dun". According to Yonhap News Agency, since January 4, 2021, "Fu Bao" in South Korea to meet the public, has been more than 1,000 days and nights. The statistics of the park also show that the total number of visitors to the panda park reached 5.4 million people, the sales of peripheral products reached 2.7 million pieces, and the video of "Fu Bao" uploaded on the social network has reached a cumulative total of 500 million views. The books "Panda Baby Fubo" and "Fubo, Happy Everyday", which record the growth story of "Fubo", have also been sold in Korea. Grandpa Jiang, the keeper, exclaimed in the book that all the time he spent with "Fubo" was full of happiness, and he believed that "Fubo" would be happy no matter who he meets or where he goes in the future.

Xiang Xiang

Xiangxiang, the daughter of Li Li, a male giant panda and Zhen Zhen, a female giant panda who went to Japan from China in 2011, was born at the Ueno Zoo on June 12, 2017, marking the first time a baby giant panda was born at the zoo in the last 29 years, making it doubly popular among the Japanese public.
Xiang Xiang', who was supposed to be returned to China at the age of two, has been living in Japan for more than five years because of the covid epidemic, allowing us panda fans to see its adorable appearance for a long time longer, "Compared to other pandas, 'Xiang Xiang' is pretty , and very lively." Fans hope "Xiang Xiang" will be loved by everyone when she returns to China and continue to be a panda star.

Ru Yi

On April 29, 2019, the giant panda "Ruyi" arrived at Moscow airport from China. The opening of the China-Russia Giant Panda Conservation and Research Cooperation Program. Ruyi is a male panda full of courage and curiosity, he is very dexterous in climbing on various stakes, and quickly find all kinds of toys hidden by zoologists. Ruyi is very active and often comes up to the glass partition to interact with the audience. Within 3 years of arriving in Russia, Ruyi had become a Kung Fu Panda, and in 2023, Ruyi became a father when "Ding Ding" gave birth to his cub.
Giant pandas have extraordinary popularity in Russia. Even a flurry of snowflakes can not stop the public's enthusiasm for viewing, the panda museum attracted a steady stream of visitors.

Yuan Meng

Yuan Meng, the first giant panda born in France, was born on August 4, 2017. The name "Yuan Meng" was chosen by the French public in a vote organized by the Beauval Zoo. The name means "the realization of a dream" in Chinese. Yuan Meng is the son of Huan Huan and Yuan Zi, two giant pandas on loan from China to France. The birth of Yuan Meng was a significant event for the Beauval Zoo and the French public, as it was the first time a giant panda was born in France.
Yuan Meng has been a star since birth, with his every move and milestone being closely followed by the public. He has captured the hearts of many with his playful antics and adorable appearance. Yuan Meng has become a symbol of friendship and cooperation between China and France, and a beloved ambassador for giant panda conservation.
